This part is fairly universal, however the upcomming portions will require you to make a choice about how you want your data.
The recommended way is to find your own host with php to process your own data, this reduces your dependency on other services, while simultaneously providing you with the greatest flexibility.
If, however, you wish to integrate another form service into your experiment, that is also generally possible but more difficult because you are using a service in a way it wasn't designed. For an example of what this is like, here is an example of a simple form which interfaces with Google Forms. However, if you want to go with a third party, I recommend a service designed to be integrated with custom web pages (eg formspree).
