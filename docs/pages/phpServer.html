First, make sure you have:

(as of June 2020)

You will learn:

  1. How to turn your data into a CSV
  2. How to email yourself a copy of the data (assuming the server you use supports this)
  3. How to combine your data into a single spreadsheet


Be aware that bots sometimes crawl various pages, this page will submit with many blank entries when that happens. You can either keep from saving data in the even that the consent radio button wasn't checked, or look into some other means of directing bots away from your processing page (eg. robots.txt). For simplicity I don't include any way to prevent this in the tutorial, it is a very rare occurrence anyway.
